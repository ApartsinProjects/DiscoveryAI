Part VI

Discovery in Scientific Domains

AI-driven discovery across biology, chemistry, physics, climate science, and social systems

Overview

Part VI applies the techniques from earlier parts to specific scientific domains where AI is already transforming the pace and nature of discovery. Each chapter covers the unique data types, domain constraints, evaluation criteria, and breakthrough case studies for a major scientific field. From drug discovery and materials design to climate modeling and economic forecasting, these chapters show how domain knowledge and AI methods combine to solve problems that neither could tackle alone.

Chapters

Chapter 48 Biology and Medicine Protein structure prediction, drug discovery, genomics, clinical trial optimization, and biomedical AI. Chapter 49 Chemistry and Materials Molecular design, retrosynthesis planning, materials discovery, and computational chemistry workflows. Chapter 50 Physics and Engineering AI for particle physics, fluid dynamics, structural engineering, and automated scientific instrumentation. Chapter 51 Climate and Earth Science Weather prediction, climate modeling, remote sensing analysis, and environmental monitoring with AI. Chapter 52 Social and Economic Systems Computational social science, economic modeling, policy simulation, and behavioral discovery.
