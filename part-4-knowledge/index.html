Part IV

Discovery Through Knowledge

Mining literature, building knowledge graphs, and generating validated hypotheses

Overview

Part IV focuses on extracting, organizing, and reasoning over the vast body of existing scientific knowledge. These chapters cover literature mining at scale, retrieval-augmented generation for discovery, knowledge graph construction, automated hypothesis generation, research agents that navigate the scientific literature autonomously, and rigorous claim validation pipelines that separate genuine findings from noise.

Chapters

Chapter 36 Literature Mining Extracting structured knowledge from scientific papers, patents, and technical reports at scale. Chapter 37 RAG Discovery Systems Retrieval-augmented generation architectures that ground discovery in verified source material. Chapter 38 Knowledge Graph Discovery Constructing, embedding, and reasoning over knowledge graphs to find hidden connections. Chapter 39 Hypothesis Generation Automated generation of testable scientific hypotheses from data, literature, and knowledge structures. Chapter 40 Research Agents Autonomous AI agents that search, read, synthesize, and reason across scientific literature. Chapter 41 Claim Validation Verifying scientific claims through replication analysis, statistical auditing, and evidence triangulation.
